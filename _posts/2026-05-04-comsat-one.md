# ARCHIVES: Kerbal Space Program debuts with LKO launch of ComSat I

###### Author: lunatrifx

It's a new era for Kerbalkind following the successful launch of one of three planned microsatellites in ComSat I. 

Aboard a simple rocket assembled by Kerbal engineer Jebediah Kerman, the payload soared through the atmosphere and was deployed as it reached low Kerbin orbit at 70 kilometers.

The microsatellite is equipped with the in-house engineered Communotron 16 relay antenna, four solar panel tiles, and a rechargable 20 watt lithium compound battery. 

ComSat I's mission is to establish the program's CommNet, and lay the groundwork alongside other LKO satellites for the future Deep Space Network.
